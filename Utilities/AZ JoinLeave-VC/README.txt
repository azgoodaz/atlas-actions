# Join to Create Voice Channels

A script that makes 1 voice channel the ability to create 1 temporary channel per user at a time.

## Usage

1) In the 'Join VC' action, under 'Voice Channel' select your 'Join to Create' voice channel.
2) For the 'JoinToCreateID' put in the id of your 'Join to Create' voice channel.
3) Press 'Save' and your done.
